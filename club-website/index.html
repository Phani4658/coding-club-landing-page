About Us

A student club of Geethanjali Institute of science and technology aiming to establish the programming culture among the campus by gathering the passionate geeks at one place.

What we do

  • Making the students ready for industry right from their early days of college.
  • Projecting The programming in a fun way.
  • Providing the best resources for the members of club
  • Hosting various programming competetions and Hack-a-thons
  • Supporting the students to participate in international level programs like Google summer of code(GSOC)
